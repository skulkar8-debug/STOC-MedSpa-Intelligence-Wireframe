How to Read This
Zone rings are drawn from the existing population center of a sub-market — not from any platform clinic. A Z4 record means that zip code has consumer demand but no MedSpa supply within 15–25 miles. That is the gap.
Dallas ranks #1 nationally by opportunity count — yet North Frisco and South Fort Worth show genuine white space. A CBSA score alone misses this. The rings reveal it.
